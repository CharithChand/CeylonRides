About Us

A Network Built on Verification, Not Volume

CeylonRides is a managed network of independent professional drivers across Sri Lanka. We don't own a fleet — we vet one. Every driver is interviewed, licensed-checked and reviewed continuously; every vehicle is inspected and insured before it carries a single guest.

That model means islandwide coverage without compromising on standards: the same fixed pricing, the same professional service, whether you land in Katunayake at 3am or need a week in the hill country.
